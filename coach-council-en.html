The HMB Schools Coaching Council is an advisory body consisting of renowned and respected fighters and members of HMB Russia.

The goals of the Coaching Council are:

  • ensuring the optimization of HMB rules and regulations;
  • creating a universal methodological program for HMB;
  • ensuring a sustainable sports and training process in the HMB Schools Federation in all training centers of the Project;
  • comprehensive development of HMB as a competitive discipline that combines both sport and historicity.
